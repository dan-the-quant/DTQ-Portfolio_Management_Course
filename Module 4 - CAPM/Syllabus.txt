Dan the Quant - Quant Investor Course

Module 4 - Capital Asset Pricing Model

    * 4.1 CAPM: Naive CAPM
        Topics:
        - Risk Free Rate
        - Rolling Variance and Rolling Covariance
        - Rolling Beta and Rolling Alpha
        - Security Market Line
        - Treynor Ratio vs Sharpe Ratio

    * 4.2 CAPM: Time-Weighted CAPM
        Topics:
        - Calculating Beta using WLS
        - The weights function and how to apply it
        - Weights: Ascending vs Descending

    * 4.3 ICAPM: Intertemporal Capital Allocation Model (Merton, 1973)
        Topics:
        - The Hedge Portfolio (Zero Beta Portfolio)
        - Calculating the Model for the whole time stamp
        - Comparing Market Beta vs Hedge Beta
        - Obtaining the Rolling Betas
        - Applying a Constraint (sum of betas equal to one)

    * 4.4 CAMP: Betas for the whole Universe
        Topics:
        - Importing the data for the whole universe
        - Calculating Rolling Betas and Residual Returns
        - Comparing Historical Beta vs Mean Beta (mean of rolling betas)
        - Histogram and Distribution of Betas

    * 4.5 CAPM: Kalman Filters
        Topics:
        - Calculating Beta using Kalman Filters
        - Comparing Standard Beta vs Kalman Beta
        - Comparing the Volatility (Standard Deviation) of the Betas
